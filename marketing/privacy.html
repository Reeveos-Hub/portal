Privacy Policy

This policy explains how ReeveOS collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Table of Contents

1. Who We Are 2. What Data We Collect 3. How We Use Your Data 4. Legal Basis for Processing 5. Data Sharing 6. Data Retention 7. Your Rights 8. Cookies 9. Security 10. Changes to This Policy 11. Contact Us

We never sell your data

You can request deletion at any time

All data stored in the UK/EU

1

Who We Are

ReeveOS Ltd is a UK-registered company (Company No. 12345678) with our registered office at 123 High Street, London, E1 6AN. We are the data controller for all personal data processed through our booking and business management platform.

We provide a comprehensive booking system and business management tools for independent service businesses across the UK, including salons, barbers, restaurants, beauty clinics, personal trainers, and hundreds of other service providers.

Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at privacy@reeveos.app.

2

What Data We Collect

For Business Owners

  • Business name, address, and contact details
  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment and billing information
  • Staff member details you add to the system
  • Service offerings, pricing, and availability
  • Usage data and analytics about how you use ReeveOS

For Customers

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Communication preferences and marketing consent
  • Notes added by businesses (e.g., service preferences)

We only collect data that is necessary to provide our service. You can request to see all data we hold about you at any time.

3

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

Provide Our Service

Process bookings, manage appointments, send confirmations and reminders

Process Payments

Handle billing, subscriptions, and customer payments securely

Improve Our Platform

Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience

Customer Support

Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, provide assistance

Send Updates

Notify you about important service changes and new features

Marketing (with consent)

Share relevant tips, offers, and updates if you've opted in

We will never use your data for purposes you haven't consented to, and you can withdraw consent at any time.

4

Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

Contract Performance

Processing necessary to provide our booking and management services to you

Legal Obligation

Compliance with tax, accounting, and other legal requirements

Legitimate Interests

Improving our service, preventing fraud, and ensuring platform security

Consent

Marketing communications and optional features you've explicitly agreed to

5

Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

We only share your data with trusted partners who help us deliver our service:

Payment Processors

Stripe

Secure payment processing for subscriptions and customer bookings. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant.

Cloud Infrastructure

AWS (UK/EU)

All data stored on secure UK and EU-based servers with enterprise-grade encryption.

Email Service

SendGrid

Booking confirmations, reminders, and account notifications sent via secure email infrastructure.

Analytics

Privacy-focused

Anonymized usage data to improve our service. We don't use invasive tracking.

All third-party processors are contractually bound to GDPR compliance and data protection standards.

6

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active and for legitimate business purposes

Closed accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days of account closure

Financial records: Retained for 7 years to comply with UK tax and accounting laws

Marketing data: Deleted immediately upon withdrawal of consent

Analytics: Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained indefinitely for product improvement

7

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)

Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@reeveos.app. We'll respond within 30 days.

8

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience and ensure our platform works properly:

Essential Cookies

Required

Keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and ensure security. These cannot be disabled.

Examples: session_id, auth_token, preferences

Analytics Cookies

Optional

Help us understand how you use ReeveOS so we can improve it. All data is anonymized.

Examples: _ga, _gid (Google Analytics alternative)

Marketing Cookies

Optional

Show you relevant content and measure campaign effectiveness. Only with your consent.

Examples: _fbp, conversion_tracking

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings or through our cookie banner. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

9

Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-leading measures:

Encryption

All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

Secure Infrastructure

UK/EU-based servers with 24/7 monitoring and DDoS protection

Access Controls

Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication

Regular Audits

Penetration testing and security reviews by third-party experts

Backups

Automated daily backups stored in geographically separate locations

Breach Response

Incident response plan to notify affected users within 72 hours

If you notice any security issues, please report them immediately to security@rezvo.app.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons.

When we make changes:

We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page

For significant changes, we'll notify you by email or through an in-app notification

You'll have 30 days to review changes before they take effect

Continued use of ReeveOS after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
