The salon industry has changed

Most stylists and therapists are now self-employed. They rent a chair, run their own bookings, take their own payments. And you — the salon owner — can’t see a thing.

Zero visibility

Everyone’s got their own Fresha. Their own card machine. You have no idea what’s happening in your own building.

Card machine chaos

Five different card machines at the till. WorldPay, Dojo, SumUp — it looks unprofessional and clients notice.

Can’t spot problems

Who’s underperforming? Who has empty chairs? Who’s your top earner? You have no data to manage your business.

One system. Everyone’s independent.

You get the overview. They keep their freedom. Payments split instantly.

Invite your team

Add self-employed stylists and therapists. They get their own portal with their own calendar, clients, and revenue tracking.

Instant payment split

Client pays £100. Stripe Connect splits instantly: £70 to the stylist, £30 to you. No end-of-week maths. No trust issues.

Real-time overview

See total revenue, bookings, chair utilisation, and who’s performing — all from one dashboard. Spot problems before they cost you.

Tap to Pay on iPhone

No card machine needed. Each operator takes contactless payments on their own iPhone via Stripe Tap to Pay. Funds split to their bank instantly.

Complete data isolation

GDPR & ICO compliant. Each operator sees only their own clients. No data leakage between operators. Encrypted at rest. Audit-logged.

One booking page

Clients see all your services and available operators on one shared booking page. No more “which Fresha link is yours again?”
