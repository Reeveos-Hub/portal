Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What does Uber Direct charge?

Uber Direct charges a flat per-delivery fee based on distance — typically £3-5. There's no commission on the food. You pay only for the delivery service, not a percentage of your revenue.

Do I need an Uber account?

No — we handle the integration. When you enable Uber Direct in your ReeveOS dashboard, we'll connect everything behind the scenes. You don't need to sign up for Uber or manage any separate accounts.

Will customers get tracking?

Yes — customers receive real-time driver tracking via a text link. They can see exactly where their driver is and get an estimated arrival time, just like they would with any Uber delivery.

How quickly will a driver arrive?

Pickup times vary by location and demand, but typically drivers arrive within 10-20 minutes of your order being marked ready. The system automatically dispatches the nearest available driver.

Can I still do my own deliveries?

Absolutely. Uber Direct is optional. You can choose to use it for some orders and handle others yourself. It's a flexible tool to use when you need extra delivery capacity.

Is this available everywhere in the UK?

Uber Direct is available in most major UK cities and towns where Uber operates. Coverage continues to expand. We'll let you know if your area is supported when you join the waitlist.
